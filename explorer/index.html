Methodology

Demographic data come from the American Community Survey 2020–2024, aggregated from 2020 census tracts to community districts. Land-use and zoning data are from PLUTO 25v4. Facility counts are from the NYC Facilities Database (October 2025) and Parks Properties (March 2026). Median household income at the community-district level is computed by Pareto-linear interpolation of ACS table B19001 bracket counts.
